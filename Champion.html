Shock Win 2020 Championship, Establish a Dynasty of Their Own

In the winning moments of the 2020 Grand Finals championship match, San Francisco Shock main tank Matthew “Super” DeLisi was alone. He was playing in a separate room from the rest of his team, but as soon as overtime melted away on Hollywood, capping off a 4-2 win over the Seoul Dynasty, he was out of his seat and dashing over to the next room to celebrate a second consecutive championship with the rest of the Shock. There were extraordinary circumstances that led to this strange moment of triumph—traveling to South Korea, a two-week quarantine, an unfamiliar facility that couldn’t fit all six players in the same room—but in 2020, one fundamental truth remained intact: at the end of the Overwatch League season, the San Francisco Shock are champions.
